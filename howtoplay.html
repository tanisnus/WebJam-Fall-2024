How to play

  1. Enter all players’ names

  2. Each player will be prompted to enter 5 dares

  3. Each player will take turns answering a true and false trivia question.

  4. If the player gets the question wrong, they will be prompted to choose another player’s set of dares. A dare will appear for the player to execute.

  5. If the player gets the question right, the next player will take their turn.
